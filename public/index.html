Rules

Rock paper scissors [...] is a hand game usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand. [...] It has only two possible outcomes: a draw, or a win for one player and a loss for the other.

This application gives a chance to play a digital version of this game:

  1. Set your Player name
  2. Pick a number of opponents
  3. Make your gesture choice
  4. Hit "Play a game" button to see who won
